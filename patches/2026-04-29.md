## GHSA-vjgj-42f6-7997 · github.com/tinfoil-factory/netfoil

2026-04-29 · Go · **MODERATE** 0.0 · Pattern: `INSECURE_DEFAULT→CONFIG`

### Root cause

The optional seccomp sandboxing in netfoil was not correctly applied because a critical system call, `unix.SYS_RT_SIGACTION`, was missing from the allowlist. This omission meant that the intended security restrictions were not fully enforced, allowing certain operations that should have been blocked.

### Impact

An attacker could potentially bypass the intended seccomp sandbox, allowing them to execute system calls that should have been restricted. This could lead to a less secure environment than expected, potentially enabling further exploitation or unauthorized actions within the sandboxed process.
